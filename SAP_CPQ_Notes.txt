What is product in SAP CPQ?

Product is an sellable items in SAP CPQ terms. 

Product in CPQ consist of Part Number, Categories, Product Type, Attribute and Price

What are type of products in SAP CPQ?

Simple product - There is no configuration. Directly add this products in catalog page to Cart items.

Sequence of Events for Simple Product:

1. OnQuoteCreated
2. OnProductRuleExecutionStart
3. OnProductRuleExecutionEnd
4. OnProductLoaded
5. OnProductReady
6. OnProductBeforeAddToQuote
7. BeforeItemAdded
8. OnItemAdded
9. BeforeQuoteRendering

Configurable products - Configurable product contains attribute and dynamic pricing.

    Example : I want to purchase Laptop.

    I have choose Dell, Need a 8 GB RAM -> $500

    Based on specification this price is different

    Sequence of Events for Configurable Product:

    1. OnProductRuleExecutionStart
    2. OnProductRuleExecutionEnd
    3. OnProductLoaded
    4. OnProductReady
    5. (Per attribute selection):
        OnProductRuleExecutionStart
        OnProductRuleExecutionEnd
    6. OnQuoteCreated (if quote does not already exist)
    7. OnProductBeforeAddToQuote
    8. Depending on operation:
    For new root item:
        BeforeItemAdded
        AfterItemAdded.
    For updating existing product:
        BeforeItemUpdated
        AfterItemUpdated.
    9. BeforeQuoteRendering

Collection of Configurable product - User configures all products in one place
    Example : 
        * Bundle Products (Parent Product)
        * Laptop (Child Product), Monitor (Child Product) & Keyboard (Child Product)

System (List) of Configurable Product - 

Parent/Child List of Configurable Product - Parent-Child product may include containers which hold Simple or Configurable products.
    Sequence of Events for Parent-Child Product:

        1. OnProductRuleExecutionStart
        2. OnProductRuleExecutionEnd
        3. OnProductLoaded
        4. OnProductReady
        5. (Per attribute selection, parent):
            OnProductRuleExecutionStart
            OnProductRuleExecutionEnd
        6. (Per container row management):
            OnContainerRowAdded
            OnContainerRowCopied
            OnContainerRowDeleted
            OnContainerCellChanged
            OnProductRuleExecutionStart
            OnProductRuleExecutionEnd
        7. (Per attribute selection, child):
            OnProductRuleExecutionStart
            OnProductRuleExecutionEnd
        8. (On saving container row)
            OnProductRuleExecutionStart
            OnProductRuleExecutionEnd
        9. OnQuoteCreated (if quote does not already exist)
        10. OnProductBeforeAddToQuote
        11. Depending on operation:
            For new root item:
                BeforeItemAdded
                AfterItemAdded
            For updating existing product:
                BeforeItemUpdated
                AfterItemUpdated
        12. BeforeQuoteRendering

Tell me about pricing in SAP CPQ

Each products can hold pricing value. It is configured in product pricing tab

Three Type of Pricing follows in SAP CPQ

1. Variant Pricing 2. Custom Pricing 3. Pricebook lookup

1. Variant Pricing - Pricing is set from different SAP System like S4HANA/SAP ERP based on Customer needs
2. Custom Pricing  - Directly added price in products pricing tab and that price will be showing in cart page
3. Pricebook lookup - It is set price based on Price books , This Price books consist of Market setup (US, Europe)
                        Also Inside Pricebook setup partnumber, price code, Price , Cost, Recurring Price and Recurring Cost

Discounting priorities

SAP CPQ, discount to customers can be provided in Quote based on User, User Type, Product, Product Type or Product Category conditions.

Discount works based on ranking like User, User Type if we set 1 and 2 rank discount max amount or max percentage applied in cart page
